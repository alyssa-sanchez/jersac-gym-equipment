About Us

Welcome to JeRS AC

At JeRS AC Gym Equipment, our mission is to provide top-quality gym and exercise equipment to help our clients achieve their fitness goals. Our knowledgeable and friendly staff is always available to assist with product selection and answer any questions.

We offer a wide range of gym equipment, from free weights and weightlifting machines to cardio equipment and accessories. Our products are sourced from leading manufacturers and are carefully selected based on their quality, durability, and performance.

  • About Us
  • Why Choose Us

JeRS AC Gym Equipment is a company specializing in the sales of gym and exercise equipment. The company was founded in August 2014 and is located in the Gyy Building, Kristong Hari, Tomas Morato, Quezon City, Philippines.

First and foremost, JeRS AC Gym Equipment is committed to providing our customers with high-quality products that are built to last. We believe that investing in quality gym equipment is one of the best ways to achieve your fitness goals, and we're here to help you every step of the way.
